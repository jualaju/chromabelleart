Girl with a Pearl Earring

Girl with a Pearl Earring

Johannes Vermeer

Year: c. 1665

Location: Mauritshuis, The Hague, Netherlands

Girl with a Pearl Earring is one of Dutch painter Johannes Vermeer's masterpieces and one of the most famous paintings in the world. It depicts a young woman in exotic dress, wearing an oriental turban and a large pearl earring.

The painting is renowned for its intimate portrayal and the mysterious gaze of its subject, as well as for its masterful use of color and light. The work has been called the "Mona Lisa of the North" for its similarly enigmatic expression.
